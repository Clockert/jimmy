Based in the Tromsø area of Norway, we offer set and customized courses, lessons, and tours for you, your family, or your specific group or team event. We are a professional team that focuses on safety first.

Maybe you'd like to learn how to react in an avalanche situation or perhaps you are more interested in the magic of backcountry skiing and taking in the view from the breathtaking mountain tops of Lyngen and Kvaløya.

Our mission is to deliver professional mountain products to local and visiting guests, generate local work, and strengthen community networks.
